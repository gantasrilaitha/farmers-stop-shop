About us


Agriculture is the backbone of the Indian Economy yet Indian farmers make less than international averages. We at Farmer's Stop&Shop are dedicated towards helping our Farmers in the best way possible. This Farmer's Portal is an endeavour in this direction to create one stop shop for meeting all informational needs relating to Agriculture.Once in the Farmers’ Portal, a farmer will be able to get all relevant information on specific subjects around his village/block /district or state
